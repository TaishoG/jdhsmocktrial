About us

Here on the mock trial team, we simulate legal proceedings by participating in simulated trials, where we act as attorneys and witnesses to argue fictional cases in front of a judge and/or jury. As attorneys, we prepare and deliver opening statements, conduct direct and cross-examinations of witnesses, and present closing arguments. We also engage in legal research and analysis to develop our case strategies and arguments. As witnesses, we portray characters central to the case, providing testimony and responding to questioning from opposing counsel. Through our participation on the mock trial team, we develop valuable skills in critical thinking, public speaking, teamwork, and legal advocacy, while gaining insight into the workings of the legal system.
